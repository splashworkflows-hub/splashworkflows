Five workflows built for firms of 6–15 staff
01 / 05
Automated Billing & Time Capture
Recovers hours of missed billing every week

Our AI works silently as attorneys work, drafting billable time entries from emails, calendar, documents, and phone logs. Auto-matched to matter codes and LEDES formatted.

  • Monitors Gmail, Calendar, Drive, phone logs
  • Drafts time entries with billing narratives
  • Matches to matter codes via Clio API
  • Attorney reviews & approves in seconds
  • Invoice auto-sent to client
02 / 05
Voice AI Receptionist
Handles your front desk around the clock

24/7 front-line call handling. Our AI answers, classifies caller intent, books appointments, pulls matter status, and routes urgent calls. No receptionist needed.

  • Answers & classifies all incoming calls
  • Books consultations with conflict checks
  • SMS confirmations via Twilio
  • Transcript + summary → CRM + attorney inbox
  • Unclear intent transfers to live receptionist
03 / 05
Dictation with Glaido
Recovers hours of missed admin every week

Attorney speaks, AI listens. Voice notes are instantly converted into structured case summaries, follow-up tasks, and client update emails. No typing required.

  • Glaido voice-to-text transcription
  • AI formats by output type (5 routes)
  • OSCOLA citations for court filings
  • Time auto-logged to matter code
  • Archived to Drive + shared to Slack
04 / 05
AI Document Retrieval (RAG)
Finds answers in seconds, not hours of searching

Every brief, contract, email, and deposition: searchable with a plain English question. Our AI expands the query, searches across the firm's knowledge base, and synthesises a cited answer.

  • AI rewrites & expands search query
  • Vector search across 5 document collections
  • Re-ranks & synthesises with source citations
  • Low-confidence results escalate to partner
  • Delivered via Slack or email
05 / 05
Contract Review: Firm Playbook
Partners review only what needs their attention

Every clause checked against your firm's preferred terms automatically. Our AI produces a severity-scored redline with plain-English explanations. Partners only review flagged issues.

  • Parses every clause: IP, indemnity, payment, law
  • Compares against firm playbook standards
  • Severity-scored issues with rationale
  • Redline assembled with plain-English notes
  • Partner accepts / modifies / rejects flagged items
